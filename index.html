Balloons – Pop!

Balloons - Pop! (Balloons) is a simple mobile game.

To start a new game, the user can press “Play”. After that, they get started on round 1 in which 32 balloons are released. Balloons each individually begin to descend after reaching the top between 0-3 seconds. The user can pop a balloon by tapping on it. To complete the round, pop all of the balloons before the time runs out or before any balloons descend to the bottom of the screen.

After a round, 10 seconds are added and the round starts with 5 balloons more than the previous round. However, from round 10 onward, each round adds 1 second more than the previous round, and each round starts with 8 balloons more than the previous round. In later rounds, the balloon descent speed will also begin to slow down by 1.65% of the speed of descent from the previous round.

When the user runs out of time or when a balloon reaches the bottom, they are returned to the start screen.

The app allows users to select an image from their photo library to decorate balloons. Images are used only during gameplay and are not stored or shared. Users select an image by pressing the “Upload Balloon Face” button. The balloon face resets after every restart. By pressing on the “Turn Sound Off/On” button, the game will toggle on and off the “pop” sound effect for balloons.

The user will have a “High Score” displayed which is based on the amount of rounds they complete, and underneath the timer they see their daily high score labelled “Best”.

Balloons - Pop! is available on iPhone and iPad.

Sea of Sharks

Sea of Sharks is a very simple but challenging mobile game played in horizontal (landscape) mode only.

The goal is to get a higher score (higher time duration having survived) as the user swims through a shark-infested sea as a little fish.

The user controls the fish using a joystick, which only has effective movement up or down. The fish will continue to move forward (left to right) at a constant pace.

The sharks vary in size, but not in speed as they approach from the right of the screen. Some of them have curved movement which launches the trajectory of the shark to go up or down to the Y-axis position of the fish at the moment that the shark spawned.

Press the green "Play" button to start. At the end of each game, the time score is displayed, but it is only stored if it is a new "Best Time"/"High Score". After a couple of seconds on the "Game Over" screen, the user is returned to the home screen from which they can start a new game.

Sea of Sharks is available on iPhone and iPad.
